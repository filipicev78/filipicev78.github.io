TOURNAMENTS

Genral Policy

Seven hills is a development first program so we do not compete in that many tournaments. That said seven hills does send the top divsion teams, so red for high school and yellow for middle school to certain tourments. Most notably the end of the winter season we host a tournament with teams from all over the pacfic northwest. Overall most atheletes will have more competetive tournaments on their high school and nation. When touraments happen they will be commicated directly through the coaches.
