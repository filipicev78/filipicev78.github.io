PROGRAMS

Program overview

We offer a wide range of different programs for different skill levels and gender divsions. Once students go to tryouts and get placed on a team they will practice and scrimmage there teams and possible compete in tournaments with them.

Winter divsion

7 hills winter program runs every sunday from novmber to march 12-13. Practice is one time a week , and every session includes drills and finishes with interteam scrimmage. It is a great oprutunity for players to improve on their skills and become more connected with the ultimate comminuty. Daily scrimmages and expert coaching are a great way for players to improve in the off-season. While mostly develpomental certain 7 hills winter league teams will compete in local touraments.

Summer divsion

7 hills Summer program runs from mid-june to september. It is a highly competetive summer league that has practices and games 2-3 times a week. The practices focus on indvuidal improvment witha focus of how players fit into teams. The summer league gives players a great oprutunity to play ultimate and compete at an elite level. With more local touraments happening in the summer, 7 hills takes certain teams to these.
