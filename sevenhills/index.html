7 HILLS ULTIMATE

Overview

7 hills ultimate is a competitive youth ultimate program for elite athetes that want to improve their skills and compete against other elite players. It takes place in various feilds in seattle and has programs for middle school and high school kids. It has practice 1 time a week in the winter and 2-3 times in the summer. It is focued on connecting the ultimate community and player improvment
