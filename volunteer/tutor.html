425 Tutoring

"When the COVID-19 pandemic began, we realized that K-6 students were missing out on their essential education. As high school students, our mission is to keep K-6 students in our communities on track and possibly ahead in their learning experience." -425 tutoring

With over 500 students and 200 tutors 425 tutoring is fully student run. They provide free tutoring services for kids k-12 across the US. 425 provides volunteer opportunities for all highs schoolers
