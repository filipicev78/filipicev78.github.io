Hospital Help

How?

Contact a local hospital, and fill out any required application forms. You can find information on volunteering at Seattle Childrens Hospital here, however, they currently have suspended all volunteering for covid.


Roles

With medical background you can work with hospital staff, spend time with patients, do some administrative tasks with staff, assist doctors during operations, and more.

With no medical background you can read to patients, deliver treats, operate the reception area, escort elderly patients, and more.
